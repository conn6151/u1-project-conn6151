Post-Secondary Scholarships

There is a variety of colleges and universities available for those who are interested in persuing a career in Software Engineering. Here's a list of some locally available oppourtunities:

York University
See More

This school's program offers students to learn about;

  • Advanced Object-Oriented Programming
  • Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  • Software Design
  • Operating System Fundamentals
  • Software Engineering Testing

- This program awards students with a Bachelor of Engineering.

- This program requires students to have a credit in:

  • SCH4U
  • SPH4U
  • MHF4U
  • MCV4U

- Students need an average GPA of 3.0 on a 4-point scale.

- Students also need the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD).

University of Ottawa
See More

This program awards students with a Bachelor of Applied Science in Software Engineering.

- Students are required to have a credit in:

  • ENG4U or FRA4U
  • MHF4U
  • MCV4U
  • SBI4U or SCH4U or ICS4U or SPH4U
  • 2 other Grade 12 university or college-level courses

- Students will need a minimum of 70% averaged in all prerequisite courses.

- Students also need the Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD).
